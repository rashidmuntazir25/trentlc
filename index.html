Our story

Where Trent LC began

Trent LC — short for the Trent Learning Collective — started with a simple conviction: understanding artificial intelligence should not require a computer science degree. It grew out of a partnership between a public university and an independent technology studio, both frustrated that good explanations of AI were locked behind paywalls and dense textbooks.

Today we work with educators, employers and public bodies to keep our courses free, accurate and genuinely accessible. Our material is reviewed by working researchers and shaped by feedback from the people who use it most.
