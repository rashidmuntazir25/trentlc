Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know before you begin — and a few things people ask once they have. Can’t find your answer? Email us at hello@trentlc.uk.

List of frequently asked questions

Just curiosity, an internet connection and a web browser. Introduction to AI needs no programming and no maths beyond school level. Building AI is easier if you have finished the introduction first, and its higher levels include some Python — but you can take the beginner level with no code at all.

Work through every chapter of a course and complete its exercises, and you will receive a free certificate of completion to download and share. For Building AI, a paid, formally verified certificate may also be available in some regions for learners who would like recognised proof of their achievement.

Start by refreshing the page and checking that your browser is up to date — most small glitches clear immediately. If the problem continues, jot down what you were doing along with your device and browser, then email hello@trentlc.uk and we will help you sort it.

We design for everyone. Our pages use clear, semantic structure and headings, work with keyboard navigation and screen readers, and aim for comfortable colour contrast and readable text. If you meet a barrier or need material in a different format, tell us — accessibility feedback goes straight to the top of our list.

It is entirely self-paced. Introduction to AI suits around twenty hours of study, and Building AI a little more depending on the level you pick. Spread it across an evening a week or tackle it over a focused weekend — there is no deadline, and your progress is saved as you go.

Absolutely — many learners find a study partner or small group keeps them motivated. You can join our community discussion space to ask questions, compare notes and swap project ideas. Once you enrol, look for the community link in your course dashboard.

You are in control of your data. Open your account settings and choose “Delete account”, or email hello@trentlc.uk from your registered address. We will remove your personal details. Please note this also erases your course progress and any certificates, which cannot be restored afterwards.

Some universities and colleges recognise our courses towards their own programmes, but that decision rests with each institution rather than with us. If you would like to pursue credit, contact the institution directly and share your certificate — we are glad to confirm course details if they ask.

We offer a guided employee literacy programme and an LMS package that delivers the courses inside your own platform via SCORM or xAPI. Tell us your team size and goals at org@trentlc.uk and we will recommend the best fit, including cohort scheduling and progress reporting.
